Design the visual layout and aesthetics for your client website.

Goals

  1. Practice translating site needs into visual designs
  2. Practice building compelling visual mockups that promote your ideas
  3. Practice CSS layout, mobile design and responsive web design

Deliverables

Due by the start of next class.

Client Site

Create additional hand-drawn sketches (and whatever else you desire) as needed for your own design process. At a minimum create the following:
  1. Create several low-fidelity wireframes of various pages of your client website to illustrate the general layout of components. Upload as images to your process page.
  2. Create a beautiful Style Tile for your client website. Post as an image or image thumbnail with link to a PDF on your client process page.
  3. Schedule a meeting with your client to present your wireframes and Style Tile for feedback. The actual meeting does not need to take place before this assignment due date, just make sure to add meetings notes once the meeting does happen.

Revisions

The following are suggested incremental steps towards moving your projects forward. They do not have a fixed due date, manage your time appropriately.

  1. Add CSS styles and layouts to your Student Page
  2. Revisit your Event Promotion and Visual Portfolio pages. Is there anything you would like to update with your new CSS skills? These should start to become compelling and beautiful pages.
